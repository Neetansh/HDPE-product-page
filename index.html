Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about HDPE pipes, installation practices and performance benefits.

What is the purpose of HDPE pipes?
HDPE pipes are widely used for water supply, irrigation, gas distribution and industrial piping because of their durability and flexibility.
How long do HDPE pipes last?
HDPE pipes typically last more than 50 years when installed under recommended operating conditions.
Are HDPE pipes corrosion resistant?
Yes, HDPE pipes are resistant to corrosion and most chemicals, making them ideal for harsh environments.
